Welcome to Udaan Seva Samiti (Birahi)

Udaan Seva Samiti (Birahi) is a free, non-profit organization dedicated to supporting students from rural and underprivileged backgrounds by providing quality education and guidance for competitive examinations.

Our organization conducts free examinations, practice tests, and talent assessment programs to help students identify their strengths and improve their academic performance. We strongly believe that financial limitations should never become a barrier to education.

Through our initiatives, we aim to build confidence, discipline, and a competitive spirit among students, preparing them for a better and brighter future.

  • Free competitive examinations for students
  • Focus on Classes 4th to 10th
  • Guidance by experienced mentors
  • Motivation and career awareness programs
